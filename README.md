👨‍💼 IBM HR Employee Attrition Analysis using Python
📌 Project Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the IBM HR Employee Attrition dataset using Python. The objective is to analyze employee demographics, job roles, income distribution, and attrition patterns to generate meaningful business insights.
________________________________________
📊 Dataset Information
•	Source: IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset 
•	Rows: 1470 Employees 
•	Columns: 35 Features 
Key Features
•	Age – Employee age 
•	Attrition – Employee attrition status 
•	Department – Department of employee 
•	JobRole – Employee job role 
•	MonthlyIncome – Monthly salary 
•	Gender – Employee gender 
•	Education – Education level 
•	MaritalStatus – Marital status 
•	JobSatisfaction – Job satisfaction rating 
•	OverTime – Overtime status

🧠 Key Insights
1.	Attrition is concentrated in a few job roles.
2.	Total number of employees in the company are 1470
3.	The company has an attrition rate of 16.12%
4.	Most attrition cases are from Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives.
5.	Most employees belong to the 26–35 age group.
6.	Employees in the Sales department have the highest average income.
________________________________________
📊 Data Story Telling
1.	Most employees belong to the 26–35 age group.
2.	Most attrition cases occur among Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives.
3.	Most employees belong to Low and Medium income groups.
4.	Young and mid-career employees make up most of the organization.
5.	Employee count decreases as income level increases.
6.	Very High income employees form the smallest employee group.
